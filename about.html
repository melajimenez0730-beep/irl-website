Who We Are

In the Room with Legacy™ (IRL) is a platform, movement & home for those building with intention. A place where the business of family, heritage, and wealth come together. Through storytelling, programs, and technology, we make legacy a living practice.

Powered by IRL Advisors, our family governance and legacy-tech advisory firm, we bridge the emotional, cultural and financial dimensions of legacy. Transforming awareness into action by equipping families, individuals, and communities with the tools and frameworks to build and preserve what matters most. We help families understand where they stand today, strengthen what they've inherited, and prepare the next generation to lead with clarity.

We begin with the Legacy Snapshot, our 3–5 minute assessment. We are also piloting NIA, the beta version of In the Room with Legacy's emerging Legacy Operating System — designed to help families assess, document, preserve, and carry legacy while we're still here.

What We Do

Four pathways. One living practice.

Every offering, from our workshops to our technology, is designed to make legacy a living practice. Our work lives at the intersection of four pathways that together make legacy real, relevant, and repeatable:

Pathway 01

Storytelling & Documentation

We help families and individuals preserve what matters most: the stories, values, and milestones that define who they are. Through guided prompts, digital tools, and curated archives, we make remembering a shared act of love.

Pathway 02

Learning & Tools

We build the practical frameworks that turn reflection into action, from our Legacy Assessment Tool to workshops and educational guides. These resources make planning, preparation, and conversation accessible for every generation.

Pathway 03

Advisory & Community

Legacy isn't built alone. We connect families, professionals, and experts through advisory programs, partnerships, and networks that sustain impact and accountability over time.

Pathway 04

Experiences & Programs

We create transformative spaces: gatherings, retreats, and intergenerational sessions that bring the spirit of legacy to life. These experiences nurture connection, clarity, and commitment across generations.

Why We Exist

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission began as a personal journey of care and remembrance.

Our mission is to make legacy building and preservation accessible to all, transforming how individuals and families see themselves and their collective power to leverage their heritage, resources, and relationships to build enduring legacy.

Vision

We envision a future where stories and values are passed down with the same care as financial assets.

A world where legacy is a living practice. Where families operate with clarity, unity, and generational purpose. We envision communities built on strong family foundations, where legacy transcends wealth to include wisdom, values, and stewardship.

501(c)(3)

Our Nonprofit Commitment

Through In the Room With Legacy, Inc., our 501(c)(3) organization, we work to make legacy preparation, storytelling, and preservation accessible to all families — especially those historically excluded from generational wealth and documentation.

About the Founder

Lauren Rosa Miller

Founder & Chief Legacy Officer

Lauren Rosa Miller is a second-generation business owner, speaker, and legacy strategist who serves as keeper of her family's 150-year heritage.

The daughter of pioneers in government and economic inclusion, she bridges business, heritage, and heart to help families move with clarity and purpose.

A global citizen and storyteller at heart, Lauren has dedicated her career to preserving what matters most — not just wealth, but wisdom and identity. She founded In the Room with Legacy™ to bring structure and soul to the way families build and sustain their legacies.
