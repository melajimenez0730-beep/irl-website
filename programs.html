Key Practice Areas

Five dimensions where legacy is most actively lived.

Our programs are built around five critical practice domains where legacy is most actively lived and strengthened. They're designed to meet families where they are today — whether you're getting started or scaling across generations — and launch you into what's next. Each program is powered by our Legacy Framework and our emerging Legacy Operating System (beta).

Flagship Program

While We're Still Here™

Our flagship guided family experience — conversations that matter most, structured and sacred.

Step into your legacy with clarity, compassion, and connection. While We're Still Here™ offers families a space to confront transitions, align values, tell stories, and preserve what matters across generations.

This is where families move from intention into structure, from silence into voice, and from legacy in theory into legacy in life. This foundational experience is the first in our program portfolio and uses the Legacy Framework and early components of the Legacy OS (beta).

Where first-generation legacy builders lay the foundation for everything to come.

Our Philosophy

Why Our Approach Works

Many legacy initiatives feel disconnected. Too abstract, too siloed, or too transactional. At In the Room with Legacy, we believe that legacy is holistic: story, systems, purpose, and transition all interwoven. By aligning programs with the five pillars, we ensure every offering connects purpose with practice, heart with structure, and memory with momentum.
