Work With Us

Partnership Pathways

Create meaningful impact through strategic collaboration with In the Room with Legacy™.

We partner with organizations committed to strengthening families, preserving heritage, and building multigenerational wealth. As IRL builds the first culturally rooted Legacy Operating System (currently in beta), our partnerships leverage both our platform and our nonprofit mission to make legacy accessible at scale.

Pathway 01

Content Collaborations

Co-create powerful narratives that amplify legacy stories and cultural impact.

  • Co-created storytelling campaigns with brands, media outlets, and cultural institutions
  • Co-produced short films or podcast series highlighting legacy families
  • Collaborative research projects on family legacy and succession trends
  • Custom content development for institutions serving multi-generational families
  • Media partnerships amplifying diverse perspectives on legacy building
We Partner With Media platforms, cultural institutions, philanthropic foundations, universities, and organizations working on intergenerational impact.

Pathway 02

Program Partnerships

Design and deliver transformative learning experiences that strengthen family legacies.

  • Co-hosted retreats, workshops, or labs blending IRL methodology with partner expertise
  • Universities or foundations sponsoring While We're Still Here cohorts
  • Corporate-sponsored legacy programs for employee resource groups
  • Co-branded assessment tools for financial institutions
  • Legacy planning modules integrated into family office service offerings
We Partner With Nonprofits, universities, corporations, churches, foundations, and community-based organizations.

Pathway 03

Strategic Alliances

Build long-term collaborative relationships that create systemic impact.

  • Enterprise licensing of the IRL Legacy OS (beta) for advisors, planners, and institutions
  • Long-term collaborations with institutions, universities, financial partners, or civic orgs
  • Municipal partnerships integrating legacy assessments into family business initiatives
  • Cultural preservation organizations adopting IRL methodologies
  • Foundations supporting intergenerational wealth building in underserved communities
  • Joint ventures developing technology solutions for family governance and storytelling
We Partner With Family offices, financial institutions, estate attorneys, government agencies, foundations, and enterprise-level organizations.
