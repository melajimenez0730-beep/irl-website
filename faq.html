Answers to the questions families, advisors, and funders ask most often about IRL and its work.

About IRL

No. IRL is not a financial advisory firm, estate planning service, or legal practice. We build the relational and governance infrastructure that sits alongside — and often makes more effective — the financial and legal planning families already have. Think of us as the operating system, not the assets.

IRL is built for mission-oriented families — especially Black families navigating generational wealth, family businesses, and legacy. We work with families at all stages of wealth and complexity, as long as they're serious about what they want to build and pass on.

The Legacy Snapshot is a free 15-minute diagnostic that assesses your family's existing infrastructure across key dimensions — values, governance, wealth, relationships, and continuity. You'll receive a personalized breakdown and a clear starting point for your family's legacy work.

No. While IRL works with high-net-worth families, legacy infrastructure is relevant to any family with something to preserve — whether that's wealth, a business, cultural identity, or a set of values. The While We're Still Here™ nonprofit arm specifically exists to bring these tools to communities with less financial access.

IRL is not therapy. We don't process trauma or provide clinical mental health services. We build governance systems — shared documents, structured processes, and frameworks for how a family operates together. We work best alongside therapists, not instead of them.

The best first step is the Legacy Snapshot — it's free, takes 15 minutes, and gives you a clear picture of where your family stands. From there, our team will reach out to discuss what comes next based on your results.
