Our Story

Built from the moment every family knows — but rarely prepares for.

IRL was born from a moment most families know but rarely prepare for — when someone who holds the story gets sick, and the silence between generations becomes impossible to ignore.

Lauren Miller built IRL so no family has to scramble for what should have already been in place. Not as a response to grief. As a living practice — while everyone is still here.

While We're Still Here™

Five phases. One living legacy.

A structured journey from family conversation to family infrastructure. Each phase builds the next — together, they form your family's operating system.

I
Gather
Bring the family into the room — physically or virtually. Set the table for honest conversation.
II
Share
Surface the stories, values, and wisdom that live in people — not documents.
III
Align
Build shared agreements: values, vision, governance, and decision-making norms.
IV
Preserve
Document what matters. Create the artifacts and archives your family deserves.
V
Integrate
Make legacy a living practice — recurring rituals, roles, and review cycles.
