Because love, legacy,
and truth can't wait.

Guided family conversations that bring peace to life's transitions, preserve your stories, and prepare the next generation — while we're still here.

What Is While We're Still Here™?

Silence is the quiet oppressor in many families.

The topics we avoid are often the ones that matter most. While We're Still Here™ is your invitation to face those conversations now: about values, transitions, love, loss, and what it means to belong.

Through storytelling and guided dialogue, families gain the courage and structure to talk about the things that bring peace, closure, and connection — while we still can.

Because preparing for tomorrow starts with honest conversation today.

Have the hard conversations

About health, legacy, and end-of-life care with grace and intention.

Preserve your family's stories

And values for generations to come.

Find peace now, not later

These conversations aren't just about loss. They're about love, living legacy, and leaving nothing unsaid.

The Experience

Five Universal Phases

Each experience is designed to honor truth, deepen connection, and build peace that lasts.

I

Gather

Create a safe, sacred space for honest conversation — whether it's one family in a living room or many families joining virtually.

II

Share

Speak what often goes unsaid. Families exchange stories, dream together, voice hopes and fears, and share wishes for how they want to live, love, and transition.

III

Align

Find clarity around values, health wishes, estate hopes, and relational intentions — ensuring everyone understands what truly matters most.

IV

Preserve

Capture the moment. Families receive guided frameworks, recordings, and tangible resources to document and carry forward what was shared.

V

Integrate

Turn intention into continuity — with next steps, rituals, and plans that strengthen family connection beyond the session itself.

Choose Your Format

Three Program Formats

In-Person

Family Retreat

A full-day immersive experience held in your home or chosen location. Facilitated by founder Lauren Rosa Miller, this retreat brings generations together for storytelling, values alignment, and intentional dialogue that helps families name what matters most and strengthen what will last.

Starting at $5,000

Virtual

Family Experience

A private, three-part virtual series tailored to your family's needs. Each session is personally guided by Lauren Rosa Miller, helping families preserve their stories, align values, and discuss transitions that bring both peace and clarity.

$1,500 – $3,000

Community

The Guided Gathering

A 90-minute virtual or in-person collective experience that brings multiple families together for reflection and guided conversation. Led by Lauren Rosa Miller, the Guided Gathering introduces families to the core philosophy of While We're Still Here™ — replacing silence with shared understanding.

$150 per family (limited to 12 families)

Who This Is For
